Having College Program for E-Sports in the Philippines

Daisy Cardeño

<<


Do you know any esports players in the Philippines? I’m sure Bren Esports comes to your mind first. In the Philippines, Lyceum of the Philippines is the first college to offer a four-year degree on Bachelor of Science in ESports. Esports continues to be a controversial subject for inclusion in different fields. Esports can be compared to chess in terms of cognitive skills used, therefore, there really should be training in mental stamina and critical thinking skills. Mental abilities also require physical conditioning for faster reaction time and developing rapport with teammates, among others.

A traditional sport has offered diploma, undergraduate, even doctorate degree, why not an esports too. Some people will not fully understand esports, but esports can also give opportunities to others not for entertainment purposes but it also gives a good profit and can be called a "profession" after they graduate. I guess through this program, people will fully understand the essence of esports and give recognition to it.
