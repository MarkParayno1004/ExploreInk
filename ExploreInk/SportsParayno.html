NBA Conquered The Pandemic

Mark Parayno

<<


Recently after the pandemic surprises the world the NBA(National Basketball Association) was closed down after a player caught the virus. But after a year NBA manage to comeback slowly. The question is how did they confront the virus? or how did they fix this kind of situation? Now I will explain on how they prevent the virus while playing the game.The NBA had a solution and is to make the player or the teams stay in one place to monitor the teams and every game the organization do not allow fans to watch the game physically but they allow to watch in live streams.
This solution was called NBA bubble where an isolation zone of players and teams. This kind of solution is brilliant because the organization can now track the players that have been infected by testing players of possible COVID-19. In the NBA bubble the teams and players are prohibited to go out individually and all the teams and players are strictly to eat on their designated area they are not allowed to go outside the bubble.
