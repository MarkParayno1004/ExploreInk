Do you know the difference between Health and Fitness?

John Marco Pangilinan

<<


Health and fitness are commonly talked about similarly but there is a difference. Foremost, health is a state of physical, social, and mental functioning in which disease and illness are absent. Exercise is one having most positive effects in having a good physical and mental health and it can help increase a person’s confidence. There are other ways for a person to be healthy and can benefit all of us. Although we all have concerns, and problems that we face in daily life, what matters is how we deal with them.
However, fitness is the state of being physically fit and well and involves aspects that includes, cardiovascular endurance, muscular power and strength, body composition, and flexibility. it’s helpful to every individual have an understanding and awareness of the difference between health and fitness.
