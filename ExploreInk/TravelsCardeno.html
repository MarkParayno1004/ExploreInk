How to Achieve Travel Goals

Daisy Cardeño

<<


Everyone of us travels is one of included in our bucket-list, and wish to have memorable experiences for every place we will visit.

Here are the simple tips for every traveller whether it’s your first or not.


  1. SET A PLAN
    It is helpful and convenient to list down all your goals or activities you wanted to do at the place. For example, in the morning you want to go swimming or trekking, while in the evening you want a nice dinner with the iconic restaurant there. Even the plan may not follow as it is because it will change depending on the situation, setting a plan gives you an option for memorable travel.


  2. DO RESEARCH
    After setting a plan, you should also research for where the places are best to do your plan or activities. Of course you should also know the culture of that place, traveling is not enjoying the place, but also enjoying the different culture.


  3. ALWAYS BE PREPARED
    Don’t forget your baggage and all the things you must have in travelling like your camera, clothes for hiking, swimming and exploring the places, and first aid kit in case of emergency. But don’t get exaggerated as if you bring your whole house. Be aware of some scammer that will exploit your money, you know what I mean!


  4. BE FRIENDLY
    Be friendly to the people in that place, people will respect you if you treat them better unless you want to be banned in that place. You will also meet other travellers, don’t be shy to join them, it is more fun to have other friends.


  5. BE GRATEFUL. ALWAYS
    At every people you meet, at every place, at every travel, be grateful. Having a positive feeling fulfills your travel goals, everyone makes everyone feel great when they are grateful for achievements they will have.
