Instagram Account You Need to Follow as a Student to Stay Motivated

Daisy Cardeño

<<


Pandemic puts us in anxiety added by the pressure of being a busy student. Instagram is a good platform that opens for everyone to share content such as tips, guides and inspiration.

Here are five Instagram accounts that students need to follow about study hacks and to stay motivated in studying.

  1. @study.notesideas
    study.notesideas shares tips and videos on different study methods, websites you need to check, apps and items you need to get, how to stay motivated and everything you need to know as a student. They also have a TikTok account, studynotesideas.


  2. @motivation2.study
    motivation2.study provides inspirational quotes and stories that will motivate you through photos and videos. They also have a facebook account, Motivation2Study.


  3. @study__motivation__
    Study Motivation encourages you to study through engaging photos with motivational quotes, they also share fun memes yet inspirational.


  4. @antonias.dailystudyblog
    Santonia Isabel is a German business and media student that shares contents on her instagram that every student would relate to. She shares a lot of things, posting content that makes you feel like she’s talking to you directly. She posts about study hacks, advice, guides, and even live study sessions which sound fun. It is definitely a studygram.


  5. @lawstudentinleeds
    Are you stressed because of studying? This law student shares personal stories and tips for preparing for exams, study hacks and how to take a break if you feel stress and burnout.
