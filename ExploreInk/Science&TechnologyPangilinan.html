Why is Science and Technology Significant in Society?

John Marco Pangilinan

<<


The world we live in today require a lot of things, science and technology has something to do with it. Science is one of the most significant medium of knowledge. It has a particular role, including a various of functions for the benefit of our society by increasing the standard of our lives. Definitely, it must respond to societal needs. However, technology has always been there to support us in our daily lives. It helps in work, transportation, medicine, household appliances and communication. Thus, science and technology plays an important role in society today and also in the future.
