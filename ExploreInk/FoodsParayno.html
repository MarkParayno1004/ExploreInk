What is Adobo Chicken?

Adobo Chicken is one of the delicious dishes in the Philippines. Itâ€™s a chicken with a mix broth of soy and vinegar and I will teach you the steps of cooking and the recipe of it. Preparing food in vinegar is no unfamiliar idea to us Filipinos. In pre-colonial times, our precursors used to cook fish in vinegar to protect their newness. Many see adobo as a twist on kinilaw, which is another customary cooking technique. Kinilaw alludes chiefly to cooking crude fish in vinegar and flavors. Another comparable cycle is paksiw, which uses meat stock in vinegar and flavors.


How to cook Chicken Adobo?

Cooking Chicken Adobo is speedy and straightforward. This formula proposes marinating the chicken to make it more tasty. It is the most ideal approach on the off chance that you need to encounter genuine Filipino chicken adobo. In the event that you are in a rush, vibe allowed to skirt this progression, however make a point to stew the chicken longer than 30 minutes to all the more likely concentrate the flavors from it. Now you need this ingredients:


  • 3-4 Chicken Breasts or Any Chicken Parts
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Oyster Sauce
  • 3-4 Potatoes
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic

Steps of Cooking Adobo:

  1. Cut the parts of the chicken by 1/2
  2. Heating the pan and put some oil, but not too much
  3. Cut the potatoes into little pieces
  4. Dice the onion and Mince the garlic
  5. Put the dice onion and mince garlic to the pan and wait until cooked
  6. After the dice onion and mince garlic is cooked, Add water and wait to simmer
  7. While the water is simmering put in the chicken and wait for the chicken to be cooked
  8. After the chicken is coocked, put the soy sauce and vinegar balance both sauce by putting 1/2 cup of soy sauce and 1/4 cup of vinegar and stirr the sauce
  9. After making the sauce, Put the potatoes and wait for 20-30mins
  10. Then let it simmer
  11. Enjoy
