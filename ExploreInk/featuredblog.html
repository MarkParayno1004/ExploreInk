May the 4th be with you

Reasons to look forward this month of May

Written by: Daisy Carde√±o

May is the 5th month of the year, and the third of seven months that have 31 days. May was named after for Maia,the Greek goddess who secretly gave birth to a son, named Zeus. Aside from Mother's Day that we celebrate every second Sunday of the month to honor our beloved mother, there are plenty of reasons to be pleasured in this month.


Here are the list of International and National days of the month of May to celebrate:


International Days in May 2021


May 1:   International Labor Day
May 2:   World Tuna Day
May 3:   World Press Freedom Day
May 4:   Coal Miners Day, International Firefighter's Day, World Asthma Day (First Tuesday of May)
May 6:   International No Diet Day
May 7:   World Athletics Day
May 8:   World Thalassaemia Day, World Red Cross Day, World Migratory Bird Day
May 9:   Mother's Day (Second Sunday of May)
May 11:   National Technology Day, National Eat What You Want Day

May 15:   International Day of Families

May 21:   National Anti-Terrorism Day, National Endangered Species Day (Third Friday in May)
May 22:   International Day for Biological Diversity
May 23:   World Turtle Day
May 28:   International Hamburger Day
May 28:   Anti-Tobacco Day, National Memorial Day (last Monday of May)
