Why eat Chicken and Broccoli with Dill Sauce?

For most people who go to the gym and workout, Chicken is the most consumed meat because of its high protein and low calorie content. Especially here in the Philippines, not all people can buy high quality Beef and Pork as recovery food because it is expensive, Chicken is cheap and incredibly good for building muscle. This is because it gives a good mix of carbs to replenish the muscles right after a workout, with a ton of protein to start the muscle building process as well. Furthermore, it is incredibly healthy for you.


How to cook Chicken and Broccoli with Dill Sauce


  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (6 ounces each)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon snipped fresh dill
  • 1 cup 2% milk

Direction for cooking:

  1. Sprinkle chicken with garlic salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat; brown chicken on both sides. Remove from pan.
  3. Add broccoli and broth to same skillet; bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat
  5. Simmer, covered, until broccoli is just tender, 3-5 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, remove broccoli from pan, reserving broth. Keep broccoli warm.
  6. In a small bowl, mix flour, dill and milk until smooth
  7. Stir into broth in pan. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly
  8. Cook and stir until thickened, 1-2 minutes. Add chicken
  9. Cook, covered, over medium heat until a thermometer inserted in chicken reads 165Â°, 10-12 minutes. Serve with broccoli.
