As per Wikipedia. Bodybuilding is the use of progressive resistance exercise to control and develop one's musculature (muscle building) by muscle hypertrophy for aesthetic purposes. Body building can be a passion or a hobby. But what is really body building? Is it only about lifting, workout, and pains? No. Body building includes the hours after workout, which is called the recovery phase. Recovery phase is as important as the workout itself because the workour only takes 1-2 hours of 24 hours while the recovery phase has the whole 22 hours. Recovery phase includes proper diet and sleep.
